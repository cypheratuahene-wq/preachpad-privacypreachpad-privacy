Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026  ·  Last updated: April 2026

✅

PreachPad does not collect, sell, or share your personal data. Your sermons and notes stay on your device — always.

1. Overview

George Asamoah ("we", "us", or "our") built PreachPad as a free application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding any information in connection with your use of PreachPad ("the app"). By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

PreachPad is designed to work entirely offline. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics, crash reports, or telemetry
  • Your sermon content, notes, or highlights
  • Browsing history or in-app activity

No account registration is required to use PreachPad.

3. Data Stored on Your Device

All data you create in PreachPad — sermons, notes, highlights, bookmarks, and preferences — is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. You can delete all locally stored data at any time via Settings → Clear All Data.

4. Microphone Access

PreachPad may request access to your device's microphone to power the Voice-to-Text dictation feature. This permission is entirely optional — all core features work without it.

When microphone access is granted:

  • Audio is processed by your device's built-in speech recognition engine (provided by your operating system, e.g. Google or Apple)
  • No audio recordings are stored by PreachPad
  • No audio is transmitted to our servers

You can revoke microphone permission at any time in your device's Settings → App Permissions.

5. Third-Party Services

PreachPad uses open-source Flutter packages and libraries to deliver certain features (e.g., PDF generation, printing, speech recognition). These are device-level services and do not transmit data to third parties on our behalf. We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

6. Bible Content

The King James Version (KJV) Bible text included in the app is embedded locally within the app package. It is not fetched from an external server and does not involve any data transmission.

7. Children's Privacy

PreachPad is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since the app collects no personal data from any users, it is safe for use by all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends on your device's security features (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We encourage you to keep your device software up to date and to use your device's built-in security features.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in an updated version within the app and on this page. The "Last updated" date at the top will always indicate the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Your Rights

Since PreachPad collects no personal data, there is no personal data held by us for you to access, correct, or request deletion of. All content on your device can be erased via Settings → Clear All Data.
